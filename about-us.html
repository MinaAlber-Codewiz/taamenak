What is

Prime Taaminak?

Prime Risks for Insurance & Reinsurance Brokers is a Saudi company that design&manage insurance policies for clients to fulfill their needs by applying the international standards and criteria. Prime distinguished team with their accumulated experience of market needs and culture can achieve client’s satisfaction.

Prime was established due to the awareness of its founders of the special needs related to the insurance industry in Saudi Arabia especially after the positive reports issued by several international and local institutions including reports issued by the Saudi Arabian Monetary Agency (SAMA) who conducted an insurance market survey in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia; which pointed out the positive growth that occurred due to the increasing of insurance awareness to the Saudi citizen and the related applied legislations which led to improve and localize the insurance industry in the Kingdom.

Our Vision, Mission

and Strategy

Prime target is to score a lead position in the insurance and reinsurance brokerage industry in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and have a distinguished added value in this market.

Offer our customers a one-stop-shop choice to fulfill their needs with full confidence and optimal risk management in order to achieve their goals and ensure their needs fulfillment.

Prime has adopted a long-term strategy based on Careful selection of professionalteam members, professional timely performance, provide accurate technical advice, reserve client rights, and provide satisfying services relating to negotiations, settlements, and amendments which effects performance of insurance policies while validity or renewals.
